The website will allow hospitals to digitize their medical records and generate analytics from them. It will also help doctors in better assessing the patient's diagnosis. The website will also help hospitals in finding candidates suitable for a new launched drug.

The website will allow pharmaceutical companies to upload their newly created drugs on the portal. Further, if the drugs are found suitable by a hospital, they can generate candidate patients for the particular drug.
